Dunmore East Boat Trips

Why Choose Dunmore East Boat Trips?

Ireland's most picturesque fishing and holiday village in the south-east, Dunmore East is a fishing village with a heart.

This is a beautiful location with the stunning scenery and sea views that will take your breath away. You're sure to fall in love with Dunmore East, a place where sleepy seaside village meets friendly lively atmosphere around a thriving harbour.

Dunmore Boat Trips offers private corporate charters on a daily or half daily basis. These trips give you the opportunity to fish on an un-crowded boat with only your co-workers, clients, business partners or friends. The charter fishing high season begins in April and ends in late October. It is advisable to make your reservations early as peak dates book fast.

Unlike many other charter boats, Dunmore Boat Trips offers the following at no extra cost: Fishing rods, Fishing Tackle, Tea/ Coffee, Soup.

The Boat

The Keltoi Warrior

"The Keltoi Warrior" Interceptor 38, is a high-tech, 38' long charter boat with a 12' beam.

Licensed by the Department of the Marine and insured to carry up to 12 paying passengers and 2 crew, the boat is equipped with a GPS colour plotter, marked with a set of fishing hot spots, Brendan will be able to bring to directly to the areas most likely to give a good catch.

Our Committment to water safety

Safety onboard the boat is our number one priority.
Safety equipment onboard:

  • 16-man Life Raft
  • E.P.I.R.B.S.
  • Life Jackets
  • Lifebuoys
  • Flares
  • Comprehensive First Aid Kit
  • Fire Extinguishers
  • Fire Blankets

What happens if the charter has to be cancelled?

  • If the skipper cancels due to weather or circumstances beyond his control, any deposit paid will be refunded in full. We will try and accommodate you on another trip where possible.
  • If the charterer cancels the trip within 6 weeks of its date, any deposit paid is forfeited.
  • If a cancellation occurs within 3 weeks of its date the entire fee is payable.
