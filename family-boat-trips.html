Dunmore East Family Boat Trips

Family friendly Boat Trips around the coves, harbour and bay of Dunmore East

Your skipper Brendan Glody will make sure you and your family are safe, have fun and learn a lot about the area and the sea.

Depending on the weather and the crew it can take different shapes:

Fishing for any age and level of experience – rods and tackle provided; fishing for mackerel or pollock; the skipper can fillet the fish on the boat for you, invariably this attracts the hungry sea gulls which then accompany the boat.

Pull out the skipper’s lobster pot – you might even get to bring home a lobster, or see a variety of fish or crabs.

Explore the many coves of Dunmore East including Lawlor’s, Councillor's, Lady’s and Men’s Cove, Badger’s Cove and Stoney Cove.

Weave in and out of Yachts and cargo ships. Get up close to cruise ships.

Along the way depending in the season you might spot nesting kittiwakes, porpoise swimming around the Flat Rocks or at the Nuns’ corner, a big jellyfish making its way around the boat and a whole variety of wildlife and interesting natural formations you can ask Brendan about.
