Your Skipper

Brendan Glody

Skipper Brendan Glody is an experiences and fully qualified skipper for over 20 years you could not be in better hands for your seafaring adventure. He was also a crewman of the local R.N.L.I for over 40 years. He is known for regaling passengers with his colourful anecdotes and vast knowledge of the sea.

Brendan's friendly nature will put you at ease immediately, weather you are a novice or a seasoned fisherman. There is no doubt that Brendan will ensure your trip is one to remember.

Feedback From Our Trusted Clients

A trip out to sea with Dunmore boat trips is highly recommended! Every day out on the Keltoi Warrior with Skipper Brendan is a fantastic experience, having spent endless days whale and dolphin watching over the years, we've had some incredible wildlife sightings of Harbour Porpoise, Common Dolphins, Minke Whale, Humpback Whales, Fin Whales and Basking Shark not to mention hundreds of seals and sea birds! Brendan Glody is an excellent skipper with vast knowledge of the area and the wildlife, he is always keen to ensure a good day is had by all with his friendly and witty manner, your in safe hands!
